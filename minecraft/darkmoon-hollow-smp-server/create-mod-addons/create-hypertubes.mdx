---
title: "Create: Hypertubes"
description: "A high-speed transport system for players and items."
---

**How To Use**

- Craft **Hypertube Segments** and connect them with **Vacuum Pumps.**
- Items and players travel instantly between hubs.
- Use **Launchers** and **Catchers** for teleport-like movement.

**Server Tip**\
Use for transit hubs or base-to-mine connections; avoid massive public tube grids.
