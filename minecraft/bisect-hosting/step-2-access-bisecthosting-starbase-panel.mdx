---
title: "Step 2: Access the BisectHosting Starbase Panel"
description: "This will help you access your server on the Starbase"
---

1. Go to the **email** **received **after completing the order.
2. On the **Panel URL**, **click **the link to the [<u>BisectHosting Starbase panel</u>](https://games.bisecthosting.com/).
3. **Enter** the credentials from the email or **log in** with the Billing Panel.
   1. You can log in with the billing panel or the login information provided in your  email
4. View the newly purchased server from the **My Servers** tab.
