## CREATE MOD FAMILY

_The mechanical backbone of Darkmoon Hollow_

### Create

**The heart of automation and industry.**

Create introduces rotational power, mechanical machines, and moving contraptions that redefine how automation works in Minecraft.

#### Getting Started

- Generate rotation using **Water Wheels** or **Windmills**
- Transmit power with **Shafts, Gearboxes, and Belts**
- Start small with:
  - Mechanical Press (plates)
  - Millstone (grinding)
  - Crushing Wheels (ore processing)

#### Power Systems

- **Water Wheels** – early-game, reliable
- **Windmills** – scalable mid-game power
- **Steam Engines** – late-game, controlled power

#### Core Machines

- Mechanical Press
- Mixer
- Millstone
- Encased Fan
- Sequenced Gearshift

#### Server Tips

- Keep machines **compact and chunk-aligned**
- Avoid infinite loops or constantly moving contraptions
- Smaller factories = smoother TPS

### Create: Central Kitchen

_Mechanical food production_

Bridges **Create** with **Farmer’s Delight**, enabling full food automation.

**Automation Chain Example**

```
Wheat → Crusher → Flour  
Flour + Water → Mixer → Dough  
Dough → Oven → Bread  
Bread → Depot → Storage
```

**Tips**

- Use Smart Chutes for portion control
- Excellent for colony kitchens and markets
